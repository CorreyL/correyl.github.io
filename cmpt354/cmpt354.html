    In this project, I was assigned to use the skills gained in writing SQL query from the course and combine it with a programming language of my choice to build a simple Client-Server Interface.

    The database that information is being retrieved from is the AdventureWorks Database. More specifically, we needed to retrieve four pieces of information from customers who bought products of the inputted color, including
    1. CustomerID
    2. First Name
    3. Last Name
    4. Highest Price (Ie. The most amount of money that customer spent on a product of the inputted color)
    We were tasked with looking into the way AdventureWorksLT stored the data in order to retrieve this information. I discovered that the architecture of the database required a Natural Join on four of the tables in the database:
    1. AdventureWorksLT.SalesLT.Product
    2. AdventureWorksLT.SalesLT.Customer
    3. AdventureWorksLT.SalesLT.SalesOrderHeader
    4. AdventureWorksLT.SalesLT.SalesOrderDetail
